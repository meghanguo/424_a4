about the dataset

This dataset originates from the Chicago Data Portal and reflects the daily volume of violations occuring in Children's Safety Zones for each speed camera. Children's Safety Zones are defined as the area within 1/8th of a mile from schools and parks. The data began collection on July 1, 2014 and continues present day.
